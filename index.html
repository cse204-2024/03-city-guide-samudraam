San Diego, California

Welcome to San Diego!

San Diego, a city in Southern California, blends natural beauty with cultural richness against the backdrop of a perfect climate. The city boasts over 70 miles of pristine beaches, including the iconic Pacific Beach and tranquil La Jolla Cove. Balboa Park, the city's cultural heart, offers lush landscapes alongside a plethora of museums and the renowned San Diego Zoo, highlighting the city's dedication to environmental and cultural preservation.

Sightseeing in San Diego

Balboa Park

Balboa Park boasts an array of museums, gardens, and the San Diego Zoo. Its rich architectural landmarks and lush landscapes make it a premier destination for arts, science, and nature. Visit their site at balboapark.org.

  • Open 24/7

Coronado Island

Coronado is resort city on the border of San Diego, known for its pristine beaches, elegant Victorian architecture, and the historic Hotel del Coronado. This charming city offers a relaxed atmosphere, stunning ocean views, and a variety of recreational activities. Visit the islands page at coronado.ca.us.

  • Open 24/7

Sea World

SeaWorld San Diego is a renowned marine life theme park known for its live shows, thrilling rides, and up-close animal encounters. It's dedicated to education and conservation, offering visitors the chance to explore marine life and learn about ocean preservation. Visit their site at seaworld.com.

  1. Open Sat to Sun - 10AM-7PM
  2. Open Mon - 10AM-6PM
  3. Open Tue to Thur - 10AM-5PM

Seaport Village

Seaport Village in San Diego is a waterfront shopping and dining complex , offering a unique blend of local boutiques, fine dining, and live entertainment. Set against panoramic views of the bay, this charming village invites visitors to enjoy leisurely strolls along its meandering pathways and picturesque marina. Plan your trip now at seaportvillage.com.

  • Open 10AM-9PM

Cabrillo National Monument

Cabrillo National Monument commemorates the landing of Juan Rodriguez Cabrillo, the first European to set foot on the West Coast of the United States. This park offers views of San Diego's coastline and skyline, along with educational exhibits, a historic lighthouse, and opportunities for tide pooling and whale watching. See more of what they offer at nps.gov.

  • Open 9AM-5PM
